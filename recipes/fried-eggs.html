Fried-eggs Recipe

Description

Fried eggs are an easy breakfast idea which can be served with bread along side a cup of tea. This is a quick fix breakfast that can be prepared in less than 10 minutes. I fry anything between 2 to 4 eggs at the same time depending on the number of persons and size of the frying pan.

Ingredients

  • 4 Fresh eggs
  • 50 ml Vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • Small chopped onions
  • 1 sliced pepper (scotch bonnet)
  • Salt to taste
  • Seasoning cube (a pinch)

Steps

  1. Heat your frying pan for a minute
  2. Add 50 ml of vegetable oil, allow to heat for 30 seconds before adding the sliced onions
  3. Add tomato/pepper as well
  4. Fry for 5 minutes before spraying a pinch of salt (to taste), then about a half of seasoning cube (I prefer less)
  5. Stir, and then make sure it is spread evenly on the frying pan and boiling in very little oil
  6. Pour in the (already broken and mixed eggs) to cover the onions, tomato combination.
  7. Allow frying for a minute before flipping.
  8. Allow another 1 minute, and flip again.

Serve with bread or eat as a whole meal
